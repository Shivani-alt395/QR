CBRN Information

1. What is CBRN?

CBRN stands for Chemical, Biological, Radiological and Nuclear. These substances can cause serious harm to humans and the environment. CBRN defence includes detection, protection and decontamination measures.

3. About CBRN

CBRN preparedness is essential for armed forces, disaster response teams, and emergency services. Proper training and protective equipment help reduce risks during hazardous situations.
